Knight

Rank: 3rd

The Knight is an incredible piece. It is the master of forks, you can jump over other pieces with it, and you can even move it on your first turn!

The reason I rank it 3rd is because the queen and the rook are just dominant.However, It is better than all other pieces to me.

Overall, I find the knight a useful pieces if you know how to play it well, but the rook and the queen are better than it.
